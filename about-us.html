Mission

To revolutionize the Iloilo City tomato industry by empowering growers, distributors, and consumers with data-driven insights and actionable solutions, fostering a sustainable and efficient e-commerce website that meets the evolving needs of the Iloilo City market, while ensuring a fair and balanced marketplace for all sellers.

Vision

We envision a future where Iloilo City tomato production is synchronized with demand, waste is minimized, access to fresh, nutritious tomatoes is a reality for all Iloilo City residents, and Iloilo City becomes a model for sustainable and efficient tomato production practices within the e-commerce landscape, with a thriving and equitable marketplace for all sellers.

Goals

  1. Enhance Production Efficiency in Iloilo City: Increase tomato production in Iloilo City by 20% within the next five years by providing Iloilo City growers with tailored recommendations based on location-specific data and insights, optimizing irrigation, fertilization, and pest management practices.
  2. Reduce Supply Chain Disruptions in Iloilo City: Optimize logistics strategies for tomato shipments within Iloilo City to reduce transportation time and minimize spoilage, achieving a 95% on-time delivery rate for all tomato shipments within Iloilo City.
  3. Balance Demand and Supply in Iloilo City: Implement a demand-forecasting system for tomatoes in Iloilo City that reduces overproduction and underproduction by 15%, ensuring market stability, reducing food waste, and stabilizing local tomato prices.
  4. Empower Iloilo City Stakeholders: Provide Iloilo City growers, distributors, and consumers with user-friendly tools and data visualizations to gain a comprehensive understanding of the Iloilo City tomato industry and make informed decisions, fostering collaboration and innovation.
  5. Establish Iloilo City as an E-commerce Hub for Tomatoes: Attract 50% of all Iloilo City tomato growers and distributors to the e-commerce website within three years, solidifying its position as the central hub for tomato-related transactions in Iloilo City.
  6. Expand TOMATAMIS Reach: Expand the website's reach beyond Iloilo City to neighboring provinces and cities within the next five years, establishing a regional network for tomato e-commerce.
  7. Achieve Seller Equity and Market Balance: Implement the Iloilo City Tomato Seller Prioritization Model to ensure a fair and balanced distribution of orders among sellers, fostering a thriving and equitable marketplace that benefits all participants.
